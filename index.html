Project Description

As part of the Study Australia Industry Experience Program (SAIEP), I led a consulting project for Georgie Boy Espresso, a boutique, takeaway-only café located on Bridge Road in Richmond, Melbourne. Known for its premium specialty coffee, fast service, and community-driven brand, the café serves a loyal customer base of commuters, gym-goers, hospital workers, and locals. With a unique positioning as a high-quality, grab-and-go espresso destination, Georgie Boy is actively seeking to grow its customer base, improve digital operations, and expand its brand reach.

In my role as Team Leader and Project Manager, I collaborated with a diverse group of students to deliver actionable recommendations tailored to the café’s business model and growth objectives. My key contributions and achievements include:

  • Conducted in-depth market research and competitor analysis to identify Georgie Boy’s strategic positioning in Melbourne’s café landscape
  • Analysed customer behaviour patterns across in-store, online, and delivery channels, highlighting opportunities to improve ordering flows and segment engagement
  • Developed strategic recommendations in digital marketing, loyalty program innovation, health-conscious product offerings, and local partnerships with nearby fitness studios
  • Led the structuring, editing, and presentation of the final business report, which received highly positive feedback from the client for its depth, clarity, and relevance
  • Strengthened my leadership, communication, and consulting skills through hands-on teamwork and client engagement

This project deepened my understanding of real-world business strategy, customer experience design, and operations within the hospitality and food service sector.

How I Turned Data into Business Impact

Define the Problem
Worked with the client to clarify real goals: attracting new customer groups and boosting digital engagement.
Gather Data
Mapped customer behavior from transaction data, online reviews, competitor research, and staff interviews.
Spot Patterns
Identified sales peaks (before 10am), most loyal segments, and bottlenecks in online orders.
Deliver Insights
Linked behaviors to growth opportunities—like targeted morning deals and expanding the healthy menu.
Present Solutions
Created clear reports and visuals so management could quickly act on the recommendations.

Insights Uncovered

86% Loyalty Morning Traffic Rising Demand for Health Options Competitor Gaps
  • Loyalty Drives Growth: Over 86% of Georgie Boy’s customers are repeat visitors, highlighting the power of a simple rewards program and a sense of community.
  • Peak Demand in Mornings: Data showed the majority of sales occur before 10AM, with gym-goers and commuters as top segments—guiding menu and marketing focus.
  • Rise in Health-Conscious Orders: Customer data and national trends both show growing demand for alternative milks, decaf coffee, matcha, and smoothies. This reflects the opportunity to further expand Georgie Boy’s health-focused drink menu and capture new customer segments.
  • Competitor Weaknesses: Analysis revealed gaps in digital ordering and loyalty at competing cafés, enabling Georgie Boy to win market share by leading in these areas.

Key Recommendations

  • Digital & Social Media: Enhanced online ordering, stronger digital presence, consistent social media engagement.
  • Customer Experience: Improved loyalty program with tailored perks, family/pet-friendly features, targeted promotions.
  • Menu Innovation: Introduced health-focused and seasonal offerings.
  • Partnerships & Community: Collaboration with local gyms, community events, charity support.
  • Operational Excellence: Streamlined workflows with automation while maintaining premium quality.
